Privacy

This project stores basic analytics to understand how the site is used and to improve results.

What we track (if you accept)

  • Search analytics: selected category, query text, and location fields you enter (city/state/postal code; optional coordinates if provided).
  • Usage analytics: when you click to contact a provider or click a provider website link.
We do not sell this data. This is currently used for internal analytics while the project is in development.

Your choice

If you choose Decline on the consent prompt, analytics events are not recorded. You can change your choice by clearing the site cookies in your browser.
